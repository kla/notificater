notificater

A jQuery plugin for displaying small notification messages.

Usage & Demo

$.notificate("normal 5 second message") $.notificate("quick 1 second message", {displayDuration: 1000}) $.notificate("offset by 64px top and 16px right", {offset: [64, 16]}) $.notificate("sticky", {sticky: true})

Rails 3.1 Usage

gem "notificater-rails" //= require "jquery.notificater"
